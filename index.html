What is DermApp?

DermApp is a web and mobile application that will help both patients and doctors by streamlining the online consultation process.

How does it work?

1.

User takes pictures of their spot

2.

User uploads pictures, which are then sent securely to their dermatologist

3.

Dermatologists can review the images from home

4.

Dermatologists and patients can communicate through our messaging service on any device!
